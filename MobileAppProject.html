Check Engine Light

  • This light means there is something wrong with the engine.
  • There are multiple reasons for this light so a diagnosis will be needed.
  • If the light is blinking, you should visit a mechanic immediately

Battery Light

  • This light means there is an issue with the battery
  • Usually you want to get this taken care of soon
  • It could mean the battery is not holding a charge
  • It could also mean there is an issue with the connection with the wires from the battery to the car
  • Ignoring this may mean your vehicle won't start next time you turn it on

Brake Light

  • This light pertains to the vehicle brakes
  • It could mean the brake pads are bad
  • It could also mean there is an issue with the brake line
  • This is an urgent light and should be addressed right away
  • You might not be able to stop soon otherwise

Tire Pressure Light

  • This light is one of the less concerning lights
  • It is also something you can usually do on your own
  • This light indicate your tire pressure is low in one or more tires
  • Simply fill up the tires until it goes away
  • Make sure you are monitoring pressure
  • Most tires state a pressure value and too much may cause them to blow
  • Too little may cause them to go flat and drive on the rims
  • Tire pressure also affects drive control and gas millage
